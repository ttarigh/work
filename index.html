Hi, I'm Tina! I’m a freelance technologist currently based in Brooklyn, NY.

My work spans two fronts: leveraging new mediums for empathetic storytelling, and innovating new artist tools.

I recently graduated from Wellesley College (2022) with a degree in computer science. I also had the opportunity to study computational intelligence, algorithms, and design at Vrije Universiteit. As a freelancer spanning industries like government, healthcare, fashion, education, and more, I feel like I’m still learning everyday.

I believe everyone has something to teach. If you want to talk or collaborate please: email me. text me.
